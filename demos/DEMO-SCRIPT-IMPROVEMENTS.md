## What Changed

The demo script generator has been significantly enhanced with:

### 1. **Stronger Opening Hook**
**Before:**
> "Hi Chris, I saw Stay Focused Fitness is a Fitness in Birmingham. I made a quick 2-minute demo showing exactly how we convert your leads into appointments."

**After:**
> "Hi Chris, quick question - how many leads did Stay Focused Fitness get last week that you never followed up with? 
> 
> [PAUSE - Let them think]
> 
> Because I just set up something for you that turns those missed leads into booked appointments automatically. Want to see it?"

**Why Better:** 
- Opens with a question that makes them think
- Creates immediate engagement
- Sets up the value proposition before showing anything

---

### 2. **Industry-Specific Pain Points**
**Before:**
> "Most Fitness businesses lose 70% of their leads because they can't follow up fast enough."

**After (Fitness Example):**
> "Here's what I see with Fitness businesses in Birmingham: Most fitness businesses lose 60-70% of leads because people call after hours or during classes when you can't answer.
> 
> Someone sees your Instagram at 9pm, calls, gets voicemail, and moves on. That lead is gone forever."

**Why Better:**
- More specific and relatable
- Uses real scenarios they recognize
- Creates emotional connection to the problem

---

### 3. **Better Dashboard Context**
**Before:**
> "Here's your dashboard. When you upload leads, they appear here. Right now I've got 5 test leads."

**After (Fitness Example):**
> "See this? This is your dashboard. I've loaded 5 test leads here. These are real leads - people who filled out your form or called but you couldn't reach.
> 
> Now watch what happens in the next 30 seconds..."

**Why Better:**
- Explains what the leads represent
- Builds anticipation
- Creates narrative flow

---

### 4. **More Compelling Metrics**
**Before:**
> "5 leads, 3 calls, 2 appointments booked. That's a 40% conversion rate. Most businesses get 10-20%."

**After (Fitness Example):**
> "Here's what this means: 5 leads, 3 calls made, 2 appointments booked. That's a 40% conversion rate.
> 
> Most Fitness businesses get 10-15% conversion. You're seeing 40% here. That's 2-3x more appointments from the same leads."

**Why Better:**
- Industry-specific benchmarks
- Clear comparison (2-3x improvement)
- More credible and specific

---

### 5. **Stronger Close**
**Before:**
> "I've already set this up for Stay Focused Fitness. Want to test it with 10 of your actual leads this week? Takes 5 minutes to set up. Just reply if interested."

**After:**
> "I've already set this up for Stay Focused Fitness - your assistant is ready, your calendar is connected, everything's done.
> 
> Here's what I'm proposing: Let's test this with 10 of your actual leads this week. If it works, great. If not, no charge. Takes 5 minutes to upload the leads.
> 
> Sound good? Just reply 'yes' and I'll send you the link to upload them."

**Why Better:**
- Emphasizes it's already done (removes friction)
- Risk reversal (no charge if it doesn't work)
- Clear, specific next step
- Easy yes/no question

### 7. **Industry-Specific Customization**

The script now automatically adapts based on industry:

- **Fitness:** Focuses on after-hours calls, class bookings, no-show reduction
- **Beauty:** Emphasizes instant booking, competitor loss, treatment bookings

- Industry-appropriate metrics
