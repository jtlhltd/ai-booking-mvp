### **METHOD 2: InMail (Game Changer - NO Connection Needed!)**

**What InMail Does:**
- Messages people you're NOT connected to
- Bypasses connection request entirely
- Up to 50 credits/month (reset monthly)
- Higher response rate (25-35% vs 20% for messages)

**When to Use InMail:**
- ‚úÖ High-value prospects (large practices, multiple locations)
- ‚úÖ Decision makers (Practice Owners, Partners)
- ‚úÖ Prospects you can't find email for
- ‚úÖ Warm-ish prospects (viewed your profile, mutual connections)

**When NOT to Use:**
- ‚ùå Low-value prospects (save credits)
- ‚ùå People you can connect with normally
- ‚ùå Prospects you have email for (email is free!)

**InMail Template (Short & Direct):**

```
Hi [First Name],

I help dental practices like [Practice Name] convert MORE of their Google 
Ads leads into booked appointments.

Most practices only follow up with 50% of leads because staff are busy 
with current patients.

I call YOUR leads within 5 minutes, follow up 5-7 times, and book 
appointments into your calendar.

Most practices see 8-12 extra appointments/month.

Would a 10-min call be valuable to explore if this could work for you?

[Your Calendar Link]

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Why This Works:**
- ‚úÖ Short (under 200 words - InMail limit)
- ‚úÖ Specific value proposition
- ‚úÖ Clear next step
- ‚úÖ No fluff (they're busy)

**InMail Best Practices:**
1. **Personalize:** Mention their practice name (not generic)
2. **Keep it short:** 150-200 words max
3. **Clear CTA:** Calendar link, not "tell me more"
4. **Send Tuesday-Thursday:** Higher open rates
5. **Follow up:** If no response, connect and send regular message (save InMail credits!)

**Monthly Strategy:**
```
50 InMail credits/month
‚Üí Use 10-15/week
‚Üí 40-60 high-value prospects/month
‚Üí Expected: 10-20 responses (25-35% response)
‚Üí Expected: 5-10 demos
‚Üí Expected: 2-4 clients
```
