## ðŸŽ¯ Testing Framework

### Test ONE variable at a time

**Don't test:**
- Everything at once (too many variables)
- Multiple things simultaneously (won't know what worked)

**Do test:**
- ONE template vs another (keep channel same)
- ONE channel vs another (keep template same)
- ONE timing vs another (keep everything else same)

**Example good test:**
```

- Group A (125 emails): "ROI Hammer" template
- Group B (125 emails): "Time Saver" template
```
