Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Booking MVP

Getting Started

❓ How quickly can I get started? +

You can be up and running in under 30 minutes! Our setup process is designed to be simple and straightforward. We provide step-by-step guidance and our support team is available to help you through the entire process.

❓ Do I need technical knowledge? +

Not at all! Our platform is designed for business owners, not developers. The interface is intuitive and user-friendly. If you can use a smartphone, you can use AI Booking MVP.

❓ What integrations are available? +

We integrate with Google Calendar, Outlook, most CRM systems, and popular booking platforms. We also offer custom integrations for enterprise clients. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Features & Functionality

❓ How does the AI booking work? +

Our AI analyzes incoming messages, understands customer intent, checks your calendar availability, and automatically books appointments. It can handle complex scheduling requests and even reschedule existing appointments.

❓ Can I customize the AI responses? +

Absolutely! You can customize the AI's personality, tone, and responses to match your brand. You can also set specific rules and preferences for how the AI handles different types of requests.

❓ What happens if the AI can't handle a request? +

The AI will politely explain that it needs human assistance and will forward the conversation to you or your team. You can also set up automatic escalation rules for specific scenarios.

Pricing & Billing

❓ What's included in the free trial? +

Our free trial includes full access to all features for 14 days, up to 100 SMS messages and 25 AI calls. No credit card required to start your trial.

❓ Can I change my plan anytime? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

❓ Do you offer volume discounts? +

Yes! We offer significant discounts for annual billing and volume usage. Contact our sales team to discuss custom pricing for your business needs.

Support & Security

❓ What kind of support do you provide? +

We provide email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 phone support for Enterprise clients. We also have comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.

❓ Is my data secure? +

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with SSL encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR and other data protection regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

❓ Can I export my data? +

Yes! You can export all your data at any time in standard formats (CSV, JSON). We believe in data portability and never lock you into our platform.
